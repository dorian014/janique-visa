The Challenge & The Opportunity

Gotham Bank has seen a significant decline in cross-border spend with younger consumers, who are turning to digital competitors.

The Problem

Key barriers prevent young travelers from using cards abroad:

High Costs 0%

Travelers worry about the cost of using cards, with 29% citing high charges and rates as a primary concern.

Lack of Clarity 0%

Uncertainty around exchange rates leads to distrust and avoidance.

Security Concerns 0%

A majority are concerned with payment card fraud while traveling.

Cash is Still King

Despite growth in travel, 3 in 5 travelers still take cash with them before a trip.

The Opportunity

Address these pain points to win back the 16-30 demographic:

Primary Travel Card

Become the primary, trusted travel card for a new generation.

Global Network

Leverage VISA's global network with a modern, fun interface.

Customer Experience

Transform foreign spending from anxiety to delight.

Long-term Loyalty

Build long-term loyalty through transparency, engagement, and superior service.

Out-of-the-Box Add-On:
Gotham Time Traveler

An innovative feature that transforms spending into digital storytelling, where every trip becomes a collectible memory.

Concept: The Trip Capsule

After each trip, the app auto-generates a visual "Trip Story" using VISA transaction data and AI. It's a personalized financial narrative that makes money emotional and shareable.

AI Memory Builder: Generates a map and timeline of purchases with highlights.

Collectible Badges: Each major trip becomes a "Travel Badge" stored in-app.

Time-Travel Challenge: Earn double XP for revisiting a country within 12 months.

Impact: Builds powerful emotional retention. Customers keep the app not just for finance, but for their memories and identity.
